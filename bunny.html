Everyone Needs a Friend Who Is All Ears

By Jessica Woods

Published On 12/17/2022

Sweet bunnies. There’s nothing like having a plump Holland Lop rabbit hippitty hopping playfully all around the house. A lot of people do not know that these smart rabbits can be litter box trained. Many also do not realize what social creatures bunnies are! They love to play! Rabbits are very intelligent and can even be taught tricks!

It is very important to feed your pet bunny a healthy, balanced diet. My bunny, Willow, loves most fruits and especially loves her low sugar snacks formulated just for bunnies but the most important staple of her diet is Timothy Hay. A bunny’s digestive system will not function properly without pure, high quality hay so make sure that, along with fresh water, is always available for your bun bun.

As rabbit owners (or do they own us? A question for another time, perhaps), we want to give them their best life! This includes fresh air, sunshine and always close supervision to protect them from potential predators. My bunny is happy to walk on some grass wearing a harness and a leash! Perhaps you’ll drive by my house one day and see me pushing my pup in a stroller and walking my bunny on a leash. Feel free to stop and say hi to me and my mini zoo!
