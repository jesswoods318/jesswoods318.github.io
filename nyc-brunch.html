Sunday Funday

While the choices for wonderful and fabulous hotels are abundant, I highly recommend trying my consistent favorite!

CK14NYC

But first, Brunch. Crooked Knife’s 14th Street location down in the Village has a great outdoor patio space that has a uniquely not NYC feel.

Oh So Refined

Refinery Rooftop in the Refinery Hotel in Midtown South, NYC is one of my and my friends’ favorite Sunday brunch spots.

A Pickle for the Knowing Ones

When one thinks high class french dining pickles aren't usually the first thing that comes to mind. But this Manhattan eatery proves that they can work together.

Coolest Place in Ktown

The Korean section of Manhattan has many novel and cool restaurants. But The Maze 32 takes the cake for me with its otherworldly ambiance.

The Best Value in the City

SriPraPhai is a Thai restaurant in Woodside Queens. Not only does it have some of the best food I've ever had, their prices are some of the lowest in town!
