The Best Travel Destination for Winter 2023

The Eiffel Tower, the Louvre, Notre Dame. Enchanted Paris!! Even in the winter when the skies may be gray, Paris still shimmers and sparkles like no other!! While the choices for wonderful and fabulous hotels are abundant, I highly recommend trying my consistent favorite!

The Hoxton is a hip and cool hotel with so many opportunities for great Instagram pics. It is within easy walking distance from some of the most trendy restaurants and nightspots. Despite very much appealing to younger generations, this gorgeous hotel dates back to the 18th century and it just exudes historical charm!
