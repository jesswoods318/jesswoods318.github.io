Oh So Refined

By Jessica Woods

Published On 12/21/2022

Refinery Rooftop in the Refinery Hotel in Midtown South, NYC is one of my and my friends’ favorite Sunday brunch spots. Located in the Refinery Hotel, the restaurant offers absolutely breathtaking views of the Manhattan skyline. The rooftop is open year round and is always filled with a very lively crowd.

A little history about the gorgeous building: the rooftop lounge is the top floor of what once was a hat factory back in the late 1800s and early 1900s. It has retained an industrial look and is now quite chic. The building is located in the famous Garment District of Manhattan, where at the turn of the century, was filled with all things fashion related.

The food at Refinery 29 is high quality, often locally sourced and downright delicious. I highly recommend their tacos which are a must have! Their brunch menu includes a classic French Toast with whipped cream and sweet berries which is simply delectable. There is always service with a smile as the Refinery 29 is known to provide critically acclaimed customer service.

If you’re looking for a real superstar experience for Sunday brunch in Manhattan, make your way down to this dynamic space!
