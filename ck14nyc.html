CK14NYC

By Jessica Woods

Published On 12/18/2022

But first, Brunch. Crooked Knife’s 14th Street location down in the Village has a great outdoor patio space that has a uniquely not NYC feel. Communal, wood park-like benches and tables fill the outdoor space which makes it easy to make friends with many of the Sunday crowd. There’s a friendly, upbeat, relaxed vibe where people gather to talk about their Friday and Saturday nights and upcoming work weeks.

How can you not order the Kevin Bacon Egg’s Benedict, if just for the name alone? The vegans will be very pleased with CK’s tofu scramble. Healthy and delicious! For those who are interested, Crooked Knife offers a Bloody Mary and Mimosa bottomless brunch option, too.

If you love munchin’ and brunchin’ to kick off your new week, make your way down to the Crooked Knife and Brunch with your besties!
