Sunday Funday

By Jessica Woods

Published On 12/20/2022

When it comes to Sunday brunch in NYC, who doesn't love a rooftop scene? Tavern 29, in the Murray Hill neighborhood, has the best of both rooftop worlds: fun in the sun in the summer and a heated enclosure for the chilly NY winter months.

Besides being a super fun gathering spot, the food here is fantastic! The Mac n Cheese is deliciously gooey and the burger is juicy and done just right! For dessert, I recommend trying the sweet waffles with berries and whipped cream. I'm getting hungry just writing about it now!

When looking for fun with friends with a rooftop vibe, check out Tavern 29 on E 29th! Good time guaranteed!
