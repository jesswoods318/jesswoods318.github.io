Award Winning Florida Beach

By Jessica Woods

Published On 12/18/2022

St. Pete Beach in Clearwater, Florida has been rated the #1 beach in the US for many years and those who have been lucky enough to have visited there, like me, know what a true gem this destination is! The beach at St Pete has the softest, whitest, cleanest sand and the always comfortably warm water is a beautiful shade of emerald green! Wherever you are in St. Pete Beach, you’re never far from great spots to eat, drink and shop.

The dining options are vast and wonderful. From beach bumming it barefoot at an outdoor cafe to the finest 5-Star Michelin dining, you’ll find it all in St. Pete’s. Fresh seafood is always the star of the menu in this intracoastal town.

Cute boutique stores, outdoor cafes with music, and lots of open air fun line the streets of St. Pete’s beach two main roads, 8th Avenue and Corey Avenue. Here you’ll find locals mingling with tourists, all in a spirited yet relaxed way.

There are seemingly endless options for accommodations at this beautiful location. Whether you’d like Intracoastal or Gulf views, or a more private hideaway, you’ll find so many choices to meet your needs. The Dolphin Beach Resort with its huge pool and fun beach bar is a personal fave!! If you’re looking to escape gray and dreary winters up north, be sure to add St. Pete to your getaway list!
